About The Event

About The Event

The world of work is evolving following the socioeconomic and technological trends shaping the nature of work, workplace, and workers of the future, both globally and in Kenya.

A combination of macro and micro factors including the war in Ukraine, inflations, climate change and the need for sustainability strategies, digital technology transformation, globalization, and increased awareness of the need for inclusivity and mental wellness, innovation, and youth employment crisis and entrepreneurship among others are driving these shifts in the future of work.

These disruptions have propelled enterprises and employers to adopt strategies that will ensure business continuity as well as productive and stable employment relations. They are changing everything from characteristics of working space, and human resource strategies to processes and other functions of the business.

The Future of Work Convention is an all-inclusive industry event focused on the identification of work opportunities for young people, entrepreneurship, innovation, and tech that are enabled by digital transformation, thought leadership, insights from experts and industry leaders, engagement with policymakers, investors and academia to rethink, strategize and leverage on the emerging trends for future of work. The future of work looks at the growth of all sized organizations with so many opportunities for collaboration

THE CONFERENCE OBJECTIVES

  • Convene key industry players to deliberate on the benefits and plight of future of work and digital economy
  • Exploring work opportunities for the youth in Kenya in the digital and green era
  • Provide thought leadership to encourage innovators and entrepreneurs
  • Review existing policies and explore favorable frameworks to the future of work

TOPICS OF DISCUSSION

  • Technology Catalyzing Shifts in the Nature of Work, Workplace and Workers
  • Innovation and Entrepreneurship as Catalyst for Work Opportunities
  • Current and Future Work Policies and Laws
  • Emerging Jobs and work opportunities for young people
  • Strengths of Inclusivity at the Workplace
  • Sustainability and Climate Change
